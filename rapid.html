Volodar Murzin won the 2024 World Rapid Chess Championship without losing a single game, defeating several world's elite players, including Hikaru Nakamura, Fabiano Caruana and Praggnanandhaa Rameshbabu. At just 18 years old, he bacame the second-youngest player ever to win the title surpassing Daniil dubov's 2018 record at age 22. He fell just short of breaking Nodirbek Abdusattorov's record, who won the title at age of 17 in 2021.
